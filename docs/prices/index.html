Live Government Data

Today's mandi prices, straight from the source.

Daily commodity rates from India's agricultural markets, pulled live from the Government of India's open data platform. This is the same feed the government publishes — we just make it easier to read. Pick a state to begin.

What you're looking at. These are modal prices — the most common trade price on the given day — reported by Agricultural Produce Market Committees (APMCs) and published by the Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare. Always check the date on each row. Not every market reports every day, so the newest figure for your area may be a day or two old. For a binding rate, confirm at the mandi itself.

Why this is here

Every other section of this site links you to an official portal. This one is different: the government publishes this data openly, so we can show it to you directly. It's the same rate a trader in your district is quoting today — you just don't usually get to see it before you reach the market.

Official

Government source

Straight from data.gov.in, the Government of India's Open Government Data platform. We add nothing and change nothing — we only filter and format.

Free

No login, no charge

You need nothing to see this. No account, no app, no fee. If any site charges you for mandi prices, they are selling you public information.

Honest

We show the date

Prices carry the day they were reported. We never present an old figure as today's. If the feed is down, we tell you and point you to the source — we don't invent a number.
